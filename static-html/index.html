The Problem

Trapped Capital, Opaque Risk

The traditional reinsurance market is capital-intensive, illiquid, and inaccessible. For reinsurers, billions in capital are trapped in inefficient structures. For investors, access to this stable, uncorrelated asset class is restricted to a select few.

The Solution

Permissionless Vaults, Composable Risk

NextBlock provides the open-source infrastructure to change this. We enable any entity—from established reinsurers to specialized asset managers—to curate and launch their own tokenized risk vaults.

How It Works

The Insurance-Linked
Protocol Stack

The Protocol

NextBlock Core

A simple, immutable, and open-source set of smart contracts on Base. The core protocol allows for the permissionless creation of tokenized insurance risk vaults.

The Curators

Risk Architects

Reinsurers, asset managers, and specialized funds act as Curators. They source insurance risk and launch their own unique vaults on the NextBlock protocol.

The Investors

Curated Yield

Institutional and accredited investors can access a diverse marketplace of insurance-linked vaults, gaining exposure to stable, real-world yield sources.

The Marketplace

Liquid Trading

Vault tokens can be traded on secondary markets, creating liquidity for a traditionally illiquid asset class.

The Infrastructure

On-Chain Settlement

All transactions settle on Base (Ethereum L2), providing institutional-grade security with low transaction costs.

Our Vision

From Venice to the Blockchain

The modern insurance market was born in the coffeehouses and trading houses of Venice, where merchants pooled capital to share maritime risk. Centuries later, NextBlock brings this foundational principle on-chain — permissionless, transparent, and composable.
